About the Studio

Crafted with Passion

Mega Digital Studio is a professional wedding photography and videography company based in Bambooflat, Port Blair, Andaman & Nicobar Islands. Founded and led by B. Meghanadham, the studio has been capturing heartfelt memories for couples and families since 2018.

"With a passion for storytelling, we ensure every wedding is documented beautifully and authentically — a perfect blend of professionalism and creativity."

Known for our fun, natural, and creative style, we specialise in both traditional and candid photography. We have covered numerous Indian weddings within Port Blair and across the Andaman & Nicobar Islands, and are fully open to destination assignments across India.
